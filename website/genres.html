RPG

RPG (Role-Playing Game) is a genre of video games where players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting. Players often control the actions and decisions of these characters, guiding them through narratives, quests, and challenges.RPGs are characterized by several key elements:

Key Features of RPG Games:

Character Creation and Development:

  • Players often create or customize their own character(s), choosing attributes like appearance, skills, and abilities.
  • Characters typically evolve and grow over time, leveling up and improving their skills through experience points (XP) earned from combat, quests, or interactions.

Storytelling and Narrative:

  • RPGs generally feature rich, immersive stories, often involving epic quests, character-driven plots, and complex world-building.
  • The player's choices can impact the outcome of the story, shaping the direction of the plot or character relationships.
